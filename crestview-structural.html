Our MMRO Capabilities

Depot-Level Maintenance

We provide comprehensive depot-level maintenance for fixed-wing and rotary-wing aircraft. Our team of certified technicians performs scheduled inspections, major repairs, and system overhauls to keep aircraft mission-ready and compliant with all regulatory standards.

Structural Restoration

Our structural repair capabilities include advanced composite and metallic repairs, corrosion treatment, and full structural restoration. We utilize state-of-the-art NDT equipment and certified repair techniques to ensure structural integrity and airworthiness.

System Integration

We specialize in integrating modern avionics, mission systems, and equipment upgrades into existing aircraft platforms. Our engineering team supports everything from initial design through installation, testing, and certification.

Quality Assurance

All work is performed under our AS9100 certified Quality Management System and FAA Part 145 Repair Station certification. We maintain strict quality control throughout every phase of maintenance, modification, repair, and overhaul operations.
